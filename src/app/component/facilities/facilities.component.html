All of our bedrooms, most with en-suite facilities, are tastefully furnished with matching accessories and individual decor. Every room has a provision for your own television. A nurse-call system is fitted in all bedrooms and en-suite bathrooms. Residents are invited to bring their favorite ornaments, pictures, plants etc, to enhance the personalized ‘my home’ atmosphere.

The home is provided with two passenger lifts, full central heating, fire detection system, free personal laundry service & mobile pay - phone facilities. Lyndhurst Park has two lounges offering different ambience's. Our garden lounge has patio doors leading to a therapeutic garden with its own wooden decking overlooking the waterfall and pond.
